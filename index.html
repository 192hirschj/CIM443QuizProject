The Symmetry Groups Quiz

Symmetry groups are an important concept in mathematics, physics, chemistry, and art. They are used to describe the properties of objects that remain unchanged after a transformation. For instance, a circle has rotational symmetry because it looks the same after it has been rotated by any angle. Similarly, a butterfly has reflection symmetry because it looks the same when it is reflected across a line.
